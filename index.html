Types of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session on a website, such as keeping you logged in or remembering items in your shopping cart.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them. They are used to remember your preferences and actions across multiple sessions, such as language settings or login information.
  • First-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by the website you are visiting. They are typically used to enhance user experience and provide functionality specific to that site.
  • Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by domains other than the one you are visiting. They are often used for advertising and tracking purposes, allowing third parties to collect data about your browsing habits across different websites.
